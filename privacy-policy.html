Privacy Policy

Bec's Happy Viewer — Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy covers the use of SMS messaging through Bec's Happy Viewer, a personal application.

What we collect

This application collects your mobile phone number for the purpose of sending text messages from the app to you.

How it's used

Your phone number is used solely to deliver messages from the app to your device. It is not shared with any third parties, sold, or used for marketing purposes.

Data storage

Your phone number is stored in a local configuration file on your own device. No data is transmitted to third-party services beyond what is necessary to deliver SMS messages via Twilio (the messaging provider).

Opt-out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You can re-enable messages by replying START.

Contact

This is a personal application with a single user. For any questions, contact the app owner directly.
